UNITREE GO2 SAFETY POLICY
VERSION 1.0

1. OPERATIONAL BOUNDARIES
The robot must remain within the designated testing area.
Any deviation from the perimeter will trigger an automatic stop.

2. SPEED LIMITS
* Indoor speed: Max 0.5 m/s
* Outdoor speed: Max 1.2 m/s

3. EMERGENCY PROCEDURES
If the robot loses connection, it must sit down immediately.
Pressing the hardware E-STOP will cut power to motors.

4. MAINTENANCE
Periodic checks of the leg joints are required every 10 hours of operation.
Ensure the battery is above 20% before starting a mission.

END OF DOCUMENT
